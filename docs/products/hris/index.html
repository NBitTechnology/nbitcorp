HUMAN RESOURCE INFORMATION SYSTEM (HRIS)

Comprehensive human resource management with our Human Resource Information System (HRIS). Centralize your HR processes & have a clear definitive workflow that will be a delight for members of your organization

Different access level providing different capabilities ensuring data is safe, secure & compliant.

Key Capabilities

Leave Application Management
Manage the leave record of your staffs ensuring greater transparency
Claims & Advance Request Management
Claims process made easy with simple flow allowing HR to disburse with ease
Job Application Tracking
Advertise the job openings of your organization. Prospective candidate application also can be tracked easily.
Attendance Management
For daily attendance, event attendance all records are managed in one place
Training Management
Manage training programs for your staffs ensuring they are constantly upskilling for the betterment of all
Performance appraisal
Innovative & transparent scoring system allowing you staffs to assess their strengths & weaknesses

Smoothen your HR workflow and improve the morale of your organization now.
